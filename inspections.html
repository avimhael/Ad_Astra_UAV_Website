Inspection & Maintenance

Drones have completely changed the way major assets are inspected, monitored, and managed. Traditional asset inspections have been arduous, difficult to plan, involved a lot of red tape and have been time-consuming tasks that often cost an organization plenty of funds to accomplish. Drone inspection will involve a visual inspection, with the drone’s camera acting as the inspector’s eyes. Visual data will be collected by the pilot and then reviewed in detail by your company professionals.

Optimize process

Perform inspections quickly and more regularly, allowing for potential problems to be identified and addressed more quickly.

seek failures

Review your organization's assets and identify areas that need crucial maintenance.

Reduce Risk

By sending a drone into a situation that would be dangerous for a person, pilots are able to collect data about the condition of an asset without having to expose themselves to potential harm.

Improve Records

Drone data represents a careful collected record of the condition of an asset over time. By archiving this data, companies have a digital footprint of the asset’s history that can be accessed at any time.

get a new perspective

Generate expansive visuals from an aerial viewpoint

Save on cost

By utilizing drones for inspections, companies can reduce insurance and one-time infrastructure costs as well as make turnaround times more efficient.

Services

Our consultation process with involve three steps: a meeting with your organization to collect requirements and design a flight route, an Ad Astra UAV pilot flying the mission with a drone asset and collecting your data, and finally the handover of deliverables to your professionals for data analysis and maintenance planning.

Asset Inspections

Assets such as bridges, railroads, power and radio towers, solar panels, and wind turbines can be dangerous and expensive to inspect for employees. With the help of Ad Astra UAV you can recruit the help of vision-based drones in order to complete maintenance safely, efficiently, and economically.

Larger assets can be up to 80m in height; inspection time with drones is cut by up to 70% over a human inspection.

The best way to protect the public and the environment is to prevent incidents from ever happening. Drone technology enhances current safety practices while producing powerful solutions.

Rooftop Inspections

Whether it be to look for loose shingles or a clogged gutter, nothing beats the safety, speed, convenience, and cost-effectiveness of a drone for property roof inspections. If you’re a residential homeowner, grabbing a trusty ladder from the shed may seem the easiest way to go about inspecting the roof of your home — but, it isn’t. Traditional methods of inspecting a roof are antiquated, inefficient, and more importantly just plain dangerous.

An aerial drone roof inspection can speed up the inspection process and reduce the risk of work accidents and damage to the client's property. It is also incredibly precise, with drone roof inspection measurements 99.4% accurate.
